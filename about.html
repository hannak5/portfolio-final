Hi there! I’m Hanna Kurian.

I’m an User Experience and Product designer with a dual background in architecture and engineering. I have always been an idea person. I’m always thinking of ideas that break open the mold of how something currently works - how can I better it? Will it work more efficiently if it is done this way? “There’s got to be a better way to do this” is always how my mind goes. Of course, applying theory I learnt in mathematics to real life problems was my favorite part of school. I know, such a nerd! Well, always been happy to be one! I discovered User Design as a field that can be pursued as a career option during my graduate program when I took a “service design” elective during my last semester. I thoroughly enjoyed the course and looked into my options of how I could segue my existing design career into the digital space. This brought me to Bloc where I honed my existing design skills that I acquired during my architecture career, coding skills from my engineering program and learned new ones that make me the better UX designer I am today. My dual background in engineering and architecture has always given me a unique vantage point where I look at problems both precisely and creatively at the same time. However, I believe my main strength is my empathy that keeps any design solution focused on the user from the beginning.
